Why FindFast?

Everything Windows Search should have been — faster, smarter, and fully private.

⚡

Blazing Fast

Results appear on every keystroke with sub-millisecond latency. No waiting, no loading spinners — just instant search powered by a SQLite index across all your drives.

⌨️

Global Hotkey

Press Ctrl+Space from any app. A minimal overlay appears front and center. Type your query and press Enter to launch — all without touching your mouse.

📁

Indexes Everything

Scans all fixed and removable drives: apps, documents, images, video, audio, code, archives, and 50+ built-in Windows settings shortcuts and 40+ system commands. Nothing is out of reach.

🧠

Smart Ranking

Results scored on a 0–7 relevance scale — exact name matches come first, then prefixes, boundaries, and substrings. Apps get a boost on longer queries so you always find what you need.

🎨

Beautiful UI

Minimal overlay with rounded corners, dark/light themes that follow your system, Windows accent colour integration, and per-monitor DPI support. Looks native because it is native.

🔒

100% Private

Zero telemetry. Zero internet access. Zero ads. All processing is done locally on your machine. Your search history never leaves your computer. Ever.

⚙️

Fully Customizable

Just type settings and press Enter to open the settings window. Remap all 8 shortcuts — open/hide, navigate, launch, dismiss, show in Explorer, open in CMD, and auto-complete. Choose between System, Light, or Dark themes. It works your way.

📦

Tiny & Portable

A single ~1.7 MB native executable — no runtime dependencies, no bloatware. Runs on any Windows 10+ (x64) machine. Available on the Microsoft Store or as a standalone installer.
